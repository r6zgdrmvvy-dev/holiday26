Spa detail

Where the spa time is strongest

Loch Fyne Hotel & Spa

Best first-night decompression stop. The hotel lists an Inveraray swimming pool, steam room, salt room, aqua jets and indoor/outdoor hot tubs. Thermal experiences are listed at £29 per hour for two people.

Best for Route 1 arrival day

The Machrie

More relaxed wellness than full spa resort. The strength is golf, beach, treatments and island calm. Dogs are welcome all year and can join you in the stag lounge and snug for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Best for relaxed island recovery

Marine North Berwick

Probably the best spa fit for Emma on the East route. Treat this as the main half-day wellness option while North Berwick golf is happening.

Best East Coast spa base

Fairmont St Andrews

Best large resort-style spa option near St Andrews. Choose it if pool, treatments, resort dining and easy downtime matter more than being in the town centre.

Best resort spa fallback

My take

Best fit

  1. Best spa route: Route 3, especially Marine North Berwick + Fairmont St Andrews.
  2. Best adventure route: Route 1, because Islay makes it feel like a proper escape.
  3. Best scenery route: Route 2, but it has the most driving.
  4. Best balance with Guinness: Route 3, because the beach stops and shorter drives are easiest.
