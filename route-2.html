Quick view

Why pick this route?

Best for

Scenery, Glencoe, Torridon, Highland roads, Royal Dornoch and Dornoch Beach.

Driving feel

The most driving-heavy route. Worth it if scenery is the priority.

Spa

Less spa-led than Routes 1 and 3. More about luxury hotel downtime and outdoor recovery.

Guinness

Dornoch Beach is the standout walk. Build breaks into the longer drive days.
