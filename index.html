Guinness-friendly planning

Dog notes

Beaches

Machrihanish Beach, Dornoch Beach, North Berwick beach, Gullane and West Sands are the obvious walk highlights.

Hotels

Dog rooms are limited. Book direct and confirm where Guinness can go: room, bar, terrace, restaurant and breakfast area.

Car days

Build in water stops and beach/grass breaks. Route 2 has the longest driving days.

Booking order

Book the bottlenecks first

  1. Golf tee times: Royal Dornoch, North Berwick, St Andrews.
  2. Route 1 ferry car space: Kennacraig ↔ Islay.
  3. Dog rooms: call the hotels direct and confirm dog policy in writing.
  4. Spa treatments: book around tee times, especially Marine North Berwick and Fairmont.
  5. Dinner reservations: especially small island/coastal restaurants.
