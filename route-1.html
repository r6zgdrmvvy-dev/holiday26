Quick view

Why pick this route?

Best for

Island feel, Machrihanish, Islay, ferry adventure, seafood and big beach walks.

Driving feel

More complex than Route 3 because of the ferry, but it feels like a proper escape.

Spa

Loch Fyne is the best proper spa stop. The Machrie is more relaxed treatment and recovery time.

Guinness

Machrihanish Beach and Islay beaches are the big wins.
